What is EPL Statistics?

EPL Statistics is a website created by UMD students that contains a variety of datasets related to the English Premiere League (EPL). On this website, you can find data relating to EPL players, matches, and team rosters.

Who is this website for?

EPL Statisics was created for English Premiere League fans as a one-stop shop for information related to EPL players, matches, and team rosters.

Who are we?

We are both programming students EPL fans who are passionate about the creation of a centralized source for information and data related to the English Premiere League.
