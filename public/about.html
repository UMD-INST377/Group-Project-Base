About Us

Since the start of the pandemic, people around the world have had to stay inside and find other
of entertainment. The majority of people turned to watching movies and TV shows. Many of these people scrolled for
hours searching for movies met their criteria, only to not find it and give up. This system will help people find
their ideal movie quicker and more efficiently. Movie Curation's system gives movie enthusiasts the ability to
use a filter to find movies that fit their desired criteria. Based on their input in categories such as genre
and favorite actors, we will sort through the movies in or database and be able to match their input with the
best movie for that user. This makes the process of choosing a movie or show much quicker compared to scrolling
through a movie watching platform and deciding on what "looks good" at the moment.
We are excited to be able to help movie enthusiasts find their ideal movie.
