University of Maryland Dining Hall Tracker

Main Page About

The issue that we as a group want to solve:

Many folks on campus are having problems owing to a lack of information about restaurants. They are uninformed of which restaurants serve which foods or when they close.

Our goal:

Our goal is to establish a website that will assist UMD students, employees, and professors in quickly finding restaurants on campus.

We can provide users with solutions to their existing problems by developing this website and harnessing data from the database. People will be able to see which restaurants serve their preferred cuisine and when they close. Our website will make people's searches significantly more effective than they are now.
