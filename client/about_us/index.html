    Project Vinyl Database


Despite the emergence of more "efficient" technologies, many people have still maintained interest in vinyl records. With the passage of time, records being damaged or lost, and the slowing of vinyl production and vending, it has become more difficult to search for vinyl records. While many rely on a collection gathered by the previous generation, or record shops that still exist in some places, it is difficult to search for these records without physically being around them.

As music is far easier to access now with the popularization of streaming platforms, vinyls will only lose popularity and become more difficult to find. Our goal is to create a search for various vinyl records, so people can gather different information on these records without having to be in-person. This will popularize the search and interest in vinyls, as more people can view and search for them in a more efficient manner.

We live in a culture focused on easy access, and hope to bring that easy access, to a technology that has fallen behind in that regard.
