Volcanoes can be a scary topic since they are seemingly unpredictable natural disasters. Finding relevant information on volcanoes in one place is difficult, and that lack of knowledge not only fuels people's fear but can also cause panic should a volcano erupt. Current websites about volcanoes are not user-friendly or visually appealing—which makes finding volcano information inefficient and unenjoyable. This website intends to increase people's awareness and preparedness for volcanoes' potential dangers.
This site aims to be friendly enough for a layperson to understand clearly and detailed, sufficient for scientists and researchers to gain valuable information about past eruptions while helping them identify volcanic hotspots. Many people are unaware they live in volcanic hotspots and are unprepared for a nearby eruption. Knowing about this could potentially save lives.
How Volcano Information helps users:
  • This site provides general information of volcanic activity
  • This site displays volcanic data in a convenient way to inform users in a user-friendly environment
  • This site allows users to visually view volcanic activity data
  • Although there are existing systems provide volcanic information, they are not user or mobile friendly
