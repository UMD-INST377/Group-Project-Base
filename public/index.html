About

Mission Statement: Washington, DC is known to be a popular tourist area for American citizens aswell as people visiting from outside of the country. A popular attraction for visitorsis to go to the famous museums DC has to offer. The number of differentmuseums and their daily visitors, transactions, hours, employees, and locationscan be overwhelming for management to sift through. Our application is to create a data set containing useful information on the museum management that includes visitor data, employee data, and facility data. This information will help museum staff manage operations more efficiently
