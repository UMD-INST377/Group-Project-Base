About Randomize

With the overwhelming amount of music released every day, it can be difficult to keep up with new music or find songs you’d like.

Randomize will generate random playlists, based on Spotify’s Global and US Top 50 Charts, to recommend you new music to try out!

Explore potential songs and the latest hits that can end up being your favorite with the click of a button. It doesn’t get much simpler than that!
