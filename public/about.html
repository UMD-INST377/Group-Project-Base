Our Mission

College rooms are known for not looking the most spectacular. No matter the decor, it doesn’t take away the mismatched furniture students end up choosing due to their hectic schedules. MyKea aims to simplify the college room furniture design process by creating a web application that presents furniture based on their preferences. No more searching on Facebook marketplace or aimlessly browsing through furniture stores. This web application includes a preference form which returns results based on the criteria users select. If users are not happy with the results they receive, they can visit the “All Products” tab and browse through other available options as well as stores where they can be purchased. Our team aims to streamline the college room redesign process based on students’ interests, saving them time that can be put toward school work!
