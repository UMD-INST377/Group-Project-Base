Mission Statement

It can be difficult to decide where to eat on the University of Maryland’s campus for a number of reasons. There are many options students, visitors or other community members may not be familiar with. Additionally, many of the on-campus eateries have various hours or may be closed due to recent staff shortages. Through this project, we hope to design an app that provides users with all their food options on campus as well as the restaurants’ locations, hours, and dietary accommodations.

While the decision of where to eat can affect virtually everyone, our group will focus on students because we ourselves are students. With a busy class schedule, sports, clubs, and jobs, it can be difficult for students to make time to eat, much less decide where to eat. Available time and the student’s current location also play a major role in this decision. When using our app, students will be able to quickly find a restaurant in a location near them with food that accommodates their dietary restrictions, allowing them to make a much quicker decision in where to eat.
