Our Information Problem

The purpose of this project is to provide detailed musical information for avid music listeners. Many music and music history enthusiasts enjoy researching the production details behind their favorite albums and artists. However, people tend to explore multiple resources to find the history and music facts they seek. This resource will contain information on albums, artists, release dates, genre, and more to give the users filterable, detailed music information.

Our Solution

This project will address an information need for music history enthusiasts by providing them with an easily navigable website containing critical details on numerous albums and artists. Rather than navigating to multiple websites to research specific albums or artists, users will benefit from the convenience of having access to a single website containing this information.
