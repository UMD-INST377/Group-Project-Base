Animal Shelters and Adoptions in Maryland

About

Our group's project is meant to simplify the animal adoption process for anyone trying to adopt a pet in the state of Maryland. According to the Washington Post some adoption centers have seen double the adoption rates than pre-COVID. Our app aims to centralize the animals of many shelter databases into a single location. In doing so, we hope to get more eyes on these animals and reduce the information incognizance and overload traditionally felt when going through this process.
