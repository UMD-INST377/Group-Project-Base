About Our Project

As climate change caused by man-made greenhouse gases continues to negatively impact the Earth, many animals are becoming at risk of being endangered or even extinct, and many animals have already gone extinct due to climate change. It is important that we do not forget these animals, as well as animals that have gone extinct due other factors. We created this application for the purpose of providing information on extinct animals in an accessible fashion. Through this application, users can view a catalog of records pertaining to extinct animals. Each animal record contains information such as the species, the average weight, when the animal went extinct, and more. Additonally, users can add new records to the database, as well as update and delete records if they wish too. For more information: Click the panda to visit the WorldWildlife Foundation's page on currently endangered animals!
