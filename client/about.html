about us

lets satisfy your hunger with our list of many hispanic restaurants

At HispanicDC, we believe accurate, up-to-date information on restaurants is important. We are objectively passionate in helping people who seek to dine at one of the many Hispanic restaurants in Washington,DC by providing the information needed to achieve that goal. Our mission is to help people satisfy their craving for Hispanic food by providing information that will allow them to do so.

HispanicDC, was founded in 2022 by a bunch of University of Maryland students in their web development class. These students had a vision to expand on their hispanic restaurant database and bring it to life in the form of an interactive website.

Review Policy

People turn to HispanicDC for searches that are critical to their quest for the right restaurant. It is important that possible consumers are able to find relevant information about restaurants that truly offer the services they're looking for.

HispanicDC takes steps to steer consumers away from misinformation. Our team makes sure that the businesses that add themselves to this website, which serves to assist consumers in finding the right restaurant for them, publish accurate information and do not miscategorize themselves. Our team carefully reviews each restaurant entry, making sure there are no inaccuracies present from the services they provide down to their contact information.

Authenticity

Consumer reviews on a restaurant are created by consumers who have actually experienced services with the reviewed restaurant. At HispanicDC, we make sure restaurants are subjected to reviews of consumers who actually spent money with them. We ask users who seek to upload a review to provide proof of receipt to verify the authenticity of the review.

Why Choose Us?

At HispanicDC, we seek to provide potential consumers accurate information on hispanic restaurants in the DC area so that they can cater to their needs and experience a good time. We take pride in battling against misinformation by providing accurate information that is verified by our team, not robots.
