About Us


We aim to make information about facilities such as restaurants, grocery stores, catering facilities, theater and stadium concessions, public and private schools, penal institutions, shelters and senior feeding program and seasonal food operations more transparent and easily available and accessible to the community. As these records are updated almost daily, it is imperative that the local public has instant access to these records to ensure the safety of the community. We also aim to use our form to make it easier to update these records to encourage inspectors to input their data in a timely manner for the community to stay up to date. All of our data is collected by and maintained by the Prince George's County Health Department.
