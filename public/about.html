About the Project

With the immene amount of information of members of Congress scattered around the web it can be difficult to navigate and find a specific member's contact methods and other attributes about them. It is a tedious process to do multiple searches and traverse different websites to find a simple answer, which can be frustrating. Our website hopes to solve all of that by consolidating information on congress itself along with specific traits on members for the best user experience!
