About

This project is about how many speed cameras are stationed near the area around DC and how they get thinner as you move outwards towards Maryland and Virginia. The cameras are mainly stationed around school areas the most. By using the search bar, you'll be able to filter out each camera based on school name. Some schools may have multiple cameras located near them.
