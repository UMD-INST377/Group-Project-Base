The purpose of this project is to allow users a quick and easy way to interact with our database of movie information. Movies are a massive part of popular culture and a pass time enjoyed by many people.

This website gives users the tools to search and filter movies by their title, rating, and genre. Once users have completed their search, they are presented with their results, including the movies' ID number, title, release year, runtime, and rating.

Additionally, a number of charts features on our home page provide users with even more information about movies, along with their associated actors, directors, and genres.
