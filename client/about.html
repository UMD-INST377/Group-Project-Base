Volcanoes can be a scary topic to deal since they are seemingly unpredictable natural disasters. Finding relevant information on volcanoes in one place is difficult and that lack of knowledge not only fuels people's fear but it can also cause panic should an eruption happen. Current websites about volcanoes are not user friendly or very appealing—making getting volcano information inefficient and unenjoyable. With this website, we hope to make people more aware and prepared for the potential dangers of volcanoes.
This site is aimed to help scientists, researchers, and safety officials—giving them access to organized information about past eruptions and to help them identify volcanic hotspots. There are lots of people who are unaware they are living in volcanic hotspots and are unprepared in the situation of a surprising nearby eruption. Having information about this could potentially save lives.
How Volcano Information helps users:
  • This site provides general information of volcanic activity
  • This site displays volcanic data in a convenient way to instruct the users in a user-friendly environment
  • This site allows users to visually view volcanic activity
  • Although there are existing systems that look up volcanic activity, they are not user or mobile friendly
