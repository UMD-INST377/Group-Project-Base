Trash Tracker PG County
This site was developed by Paris and Yabing for INST377: Dynamic Web Applications at UMD.
How to use this website:
  1. click the button to visualize where trash is being dumped in Prince George's County.
  2. you can click the button again to show other trash dumping locations.
  3. see the graph on the bottom right to see the reported dates of trash dumping.
