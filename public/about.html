Our Mission

Our team’s site aims to help prospective college students during their college search and decision process. Users are able to search for schools within the BIG10 which align closest with their preferred school size, admission requirements, and other categories.


Users can also enter schools of their choice on our site along with the school’s info such as location, average test scores, and other details. User school entries will be stored in a separate schools database which consists of school outside of the BIG10. This separate user database is available for users to make their own contributions to our site.
