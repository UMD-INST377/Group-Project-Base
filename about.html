Living By The Three


Does shooting the 3 point shot more than any other shot in basketball a winning formula for NBA teams?

This site aims to look at 3 point shot data for NBA championship teams to see how 3pt shooting effects success.

To vizulise this data the library charts.js is used to display a bar chart for the last 5 championship years.

This site also will take in user input to look for team statistics for nonchampion teams between 2015 to 2022
