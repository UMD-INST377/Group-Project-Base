What is our app?

With the overwhelming amount of music released, it can be difficult to determine which songs are worth listening to based on personal taste.

[The Spotify Music Generator] will generate random playlists based on Spotify’s Global and US Top 50 Charts and other various user input.

Explore new music that other users are adding and the latest hits with the click of a button. It doesn’t get much simpler than that!

How to Use

  1. Hit the "Get Started" Button.
  2. Decide whether you want to start off with a completely randomized playlist or create your own using the current top hits we have available.
  3. Edit and share your playlist!
